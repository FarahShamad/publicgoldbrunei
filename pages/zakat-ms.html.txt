<h2>Zakat on Gold</h2>
<p>Zakat is a purification of wealth and is wajib when gold savings reach the nisab and have been held for one hijri year.</p>

<p><strong>Nisab:</strong> 85 grams of gold.</p>
<p><strong>Rate:</strong> 2.5% (1/40) of total gold value.</p>
